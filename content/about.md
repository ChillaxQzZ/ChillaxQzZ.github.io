I am a graduate student majoring in Biomedical Engineering. 
In this era where life sciences and artificial intelligence intersect, my core research direction is to provide two-dimensional visualization data that is compatible with big language models and visual big models. I am committed to exploring how to accurately and undistorted "reduce" complex and intricate 3D medical imaging data into 2D visual features that are easy for artificial intelligence to understand and learn.
